Accreditation

Al-Andalus National Schools obtained international academic accreditation from the Advanced Organization in Educational Quality

Accreditation means that the educational programs and services and employees of Al-Andalus National Schools and their educational buildings meet or exceed the basic standards for the quality of education. The validity of this quality was verified through a self-study carried out by the schools. A committee from the Advanced organization also visited the schools to develop a final evaluation for them. They passed it successfully and declared that the schools had met the quality standards. Thus, these schools gained international recognition for their quality, and the equivalency of diplomas and certificates to other accredited schools.

Renewal of international accreditation

A committee from Advanced International visited Al-Andalus National Schools five years after the schools obtained international accreditation, and after ensuring that the schools applied all international standards and working on continuous improvement plans, the accreditation was renewed for the next five years.
