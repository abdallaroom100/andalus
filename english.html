Strategic areas

Scientific excellence

What we mean by this is: the institution’s achievement of high-quality educational outcomes, according to approved or agreed-upon standards in the fields of knowledge and skills, through qualified cadres in an environment that stimulates and supports creativity.

Value education

By this we mean: The institution builds a value system that supports virtues and noble behaviors, and is keen to instill them in the souls of those concerned.

Operating efficiency

By this we mean: the institution’s ability to make good use of the available human and material resources. To achieve the desired results in the least time

Attractive environment

By this we mean: meeting the needs and aspirations of stakeholders, both material (buildings, facilities...) and moral (freedom, appreciation, respect...) in a way that supports excellence and develops customer relationships.
