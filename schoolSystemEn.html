outcomes

Al-Andalus National Schools are working to prepare a generation that possesses 21st century skills, which are: learning skills, thinking skills, social skills, technical skills, leadership skills, self-management skills

Al-Andalus Schools aspires for the learner to acquire values and skills from every educational program it offers. Al-Andalus Schools has sought to diversify educational programs to cover the targeted values and skills.

Educational paths

The Saudi National Path

Schools are distinguished by their multiple educational tracks, in addition to teaching the Saudi curriculum as a basic track in schools, from which optional subsystems emerge: smart classes and Quranic classes.

الفصصول الذكيه

It begins in the fourth grade of primary school and means learning using a computer that the student receives and downloads all the materials.

Holy Quran memorization course for girls in Jeddah

It is an intensive course for memorizing the Book of God that begins from the first grade of primary school to the third grade of middle school, provided that the student graduates from the middle stage having completely memorized the Book of God.

American diploma

The American Diploma is one of the international educational systems. It is an educational path in which American curricula are taught, according to international standards, Common Core Standards, aiming to prepare a student capable of facing the challenges of university education and future professional success.

Egyptian track of languages

In this track, mathematics and science subjects are taught in English, in addition to teaching two levels of English, the regular level and the advanced level, as well as subjects taught in Arabic such as social studies, religious education, the Arabic language, and the Holy Quran.

Mawhib path

Mawhiba initiative to partner with schools aims to create a high-quality educational environment for gifted students. Students are selected after passing intelligence tests and other tests to measure their abilities in coordination with the National Center for Measurement and Evaluation.

Edu System

Motor skills and self-management

Self-management programs begin by exploring the learner’s talents, inclinations, and trends according to international tests and standards, and then he is given the opportunity to develop those potentials through activities, school teams, and special programs.

Keygen for cooperative learning

Keygen is the key to learning, which is the true meaning of interaction. It combines educational achievement and skills acquisition, in a modeled way that is valid for every academic content and every academic stage, that stimulates the learner’s motivation to cooperate and participate in an atmosphere of fun and enjoyment.

Mental arithmetic program

It aims to awaken the latent genius in the child, opens up to the learner the horizons of scientific and practical excellence, improves his mental abilities by performing many mathematical operations quickly and skillfully without using a calculator, and helps him achieve better academic achievement.

Google Education for the Future

Offering an in-depth look at project-based learning, it provides the information and tools a teacher needs to effectively integrate technology into the curriculum, with an emphasis on problem solving, and critical thinking.

STEAM and mechatronics

An advanced professional program that combines the sciences of engineering, mechanics, computer science, systems management, electronics, and humanities, enabling the learner to master the principles of designing and inventing smart machines and devices that serve all areas of life.
